A little bit about me

Thank you for visiting my website! I made this site to show off my projects, as well as for employers to gather more information about me. I am currently a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I will be receiving a B.S. in Computer Engineering, as well as a B.S. in Computer Science come fall 2017. My hobbies include: hunting, fishing, basketball, football, and robotics.
